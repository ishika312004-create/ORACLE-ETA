# 🍽 ORACLE ETA – AI Delivery Time Predictor  

## 🚀 Project Overview  
ORACLE ETA is a *Machine Learning + Flask web app* that predicts food delivery times, inspired by platforms like *Zomato, Swiggy, and Blinkit*.  
It takes into account *distance, restaurant prep time, and traffic conditions* to estimate when your order will arrive.  

This project showcases a mix of *data analytics, machine learning, and product thinking* – perfect for resumes and portfolios.  

---

## ⚙ Tech Stack  
- *Python 3*  
- *Flask* (web app)  
- *Pandas* (data handling)  
- *Scikit-learn* (ML model)
